About Us

Training and Placement Events

Training and Placement Events

Get a clear picture of the Industry requirements and expectations and the same is highlighted through different kinds of Orientation Programs. Special Training is provided to the students according to the needs of the Companies. Periodical Training is being given to the students, focusing on Quantitative Aptitude, Written Test, Online Assessment Test, Group Discussion and Personal Interview. Mock Interviews are being conducted to cast out the fear of facing interviews. Students are also trained to write their own Curriculum Vitae and covering letter. Training is being provided for UPSC, NET and BSRB for students aspiring for government jobs. The division helps the students to procure their Passport, Pan Card, Driving License and open Bank accounts. Guidance to apply for government scholarships from time to time is being given to the students.

Placement Processing
